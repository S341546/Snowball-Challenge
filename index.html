Hunger tracker

Predicting and preventing hunger

General idea

Hunger tracker is a software that collects data like medical records, finincial standing, job status and social status to try to predict if a family is in danger of needing aid. How it works

The software makes connections such as: the parent with the main income is at the hospital with a broken shoulder, the same person has a very physical job and is therefor at risk of loosing their income. Other situations can be: parents getting a divorce and one of them is without an income, or a family with more children than the parents can care for with their income. With enough information the software can help predict if a family needs aid, and what cind of aid. This can help governments to spend less on aid by helping early, for example by helping people get jobs instead of having to pay out money a few months later.
